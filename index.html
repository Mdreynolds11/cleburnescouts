Why Scouting

More Than a Uniform

Scouting in Cleburne is about building kids who are ready for the real world — confident, capable, and connected to their community.

🏕

Outdoor Adventure

Camping, hiking, canoeing — real skills learned in the real world, not a classroom.

🧭

Leadership

Scouts lead patrols, plan trips, and make decisions. They learn by doing.

🤝

Community

Service projects, flag ceremonies, and giving back to Cleburne — scouts show up.

⚜

Character

The Scout Oath and Law aren't just words — they're how our scouts live every day.
